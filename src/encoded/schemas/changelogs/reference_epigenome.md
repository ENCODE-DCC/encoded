### Schema version 15

### Schema version 14

* Replace *started* enum in *status* with *in progress*.

### Schema version 13

* Remove *proposed* from *status* enum (*dataset* mixin).

### Schema version 12

* *alternate_accessions* now must match accession format, "ENCSR..." or "TSTSR..."
